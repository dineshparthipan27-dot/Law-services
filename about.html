Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions clients ask before hiring our legal representation.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

We charge a nominal fee for the initial consultation to deeply understand your case and provide actionable legal advice right from the first meeting.

How long will my case take to resolve?

The timeline depends entirely on the nature of the case and the court schedules. While out-of-court settlements take a few weeks, litigation can take months or years. We will give you a realistic timeline during your consultation.

Will my information remain confidential?

Absolutely. Under the attorney-client privilege, all documents, conversations, and details shared with our firm are kept strictly confidential.

How do you charge for your services?

We offer transparent billing. Depending on the case, we work on a flat fee structure, hourly rates, or a retainer basis for corporate clients. All costs are discussed upfront.
