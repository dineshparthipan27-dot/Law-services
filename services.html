Real Estate

Ancestral Property Partition

The Challenge

Our client was denied their rightful share of a 50-year-old ancestral estate by siblings due to a forged unregistered will.

Our Solution

Through forensic document analysis and cross-examination of witnesses, we proved the will was fabricated. The High Court ordered an equal partition of the ₹15 Cr property.
